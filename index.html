Intro

Hi everyone,
I'm a 26 years old data professional from Brazil, currently traveling through Europe to improve my English and technical skills. With over four years of experience, I specialize in designing scalable data architectures and end-to-end ETL pipelines for both cloud (AWS) and on-premises environments.

I build robust data warehouses, lakehouses, and models that turn complex data into actionable insights, driving informed decision-making. Proficient in Python, SQL, and tools like Apache Spark, Airflow, Trino, and Superset, I ensure efficient data processing and analytics. Beyond engineering, I excel in data analysis and BI strategies, creating dynamic dashboards with Power BI and Qlik Sense that fuel business growth.
